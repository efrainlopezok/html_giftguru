Track Events and Interest

Gift Docket makes it easy to track all the personal interests and important event dates for the important people in your life. Then ahead of events, Gift Docket will remind you it’s time to start planning to make a great impression!

Discover and Log Gift Ideas

For each event, we will deliver curated gift ideas to your inbox and user portal that match the interests of your recipients. Have your own ideas? Easily log and track them so you are ready when each event comes!

Gifts, Cards, and Outreach

Once you’ve selected that great idea, we make purchase easy through our network of partners! Giving a card or personal outreach? We’ve got you covered there too, with great options and event reminders. Make a great impression every time!

Our mission – personal connections and giving back!

Personal connections with family and friends are among the most important things in our lives. At Gift Docket, our goal is to ensure you make a great impression for every important event in the lives of those important to you. Whether a gift, greeting card, or just a phone call, we’ll ensure you’re ready. We are also passionate about giving back, which is why we give a portion of proceeds to groups who support bringing spirit and celebration to the lives of those less fortunate.
